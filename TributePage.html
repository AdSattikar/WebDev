Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man Of India

Timeline & Achievemnets of Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1954 - Graduated with a degree in Physics
  • 1960 - Designed a small helicopter for the Indian army at Defense Research and Development Organisation (DRDO),
  • 1969 - Transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) where he was the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III) which successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in near-earth orbit in July 1980
  • Honored by the Government of India with the Padma Bhushan in 1981 and the Padma Vibhushan in 1990.
  • 1997 - Honoured with the Bharat Ratna
  • 2009 - first Asian to be honoured with the Hoover Medal which is America's top engineering prize
  • 2002 - Elected as the 11th president of India
